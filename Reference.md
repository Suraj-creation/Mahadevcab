# BS Travels Website - Improved Structure \& Purpose Analysis

Based on my analysis of the BS Travels website and industry best practices, here's a **purpose-driven, well-structured plan** that addresses the poor design issues:

## ğŸ¯ **WEBSITE PURPOSE \& CORE OBJECTIVES**

### Primary Purpose

**Convert visitors into customers by making cab booking as simple and trustworthy as possible**

### Business Goals

1. **Generate bookings** - Delhi sightseeing tours \& outstation trips
2. **Build trust** - Show professionalism and reliability
3. **Clear pricing** - Eliminate confusion and hesitation
4. **Mobile-first** - Most users will book on mobile
5. **Quick contact** - Multiple ways to reach (call, WhatsApp, form)

### Target Users

- **Tourists** visiting Delhi (domestic \& international)
- **Families** planning day trips
- **Corporate travelers** needing reliable transport
- **Age group**: 25-55 years old
- **Tech comfort**: Basic to intermediate
